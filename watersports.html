Our Famous Water Sports Activities in Goa

1. Scuba Diving


Scuba is an acronym for ‘Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus’. This is a type of activity that will let you dive deep into the ocean allowing you to experience the magnificent underwater environment and marine life. The best time to dive depends upon the stable weather conditions, where the water visibility needs to be clear. The best times and seasons to enjoy the scuba diving vary according to the locations.

The best locations to explore in Goa: Grande Island, Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Uma Guma Reef, Grande Banks, Malvan Island, and Nagoa.

Perfect season: Mid-October to Mid-April.

Approximate cost: Rs 2,000 – 4,000

2. Jet Ski


Jet Ski is an activity that can be experienced by both beginners and experienced riders, and it gives you the adrenaline rush as it generates a jet stream of water due to being compact and utilizing a propeller system. This is a sit-down jet ski that can accommodate up to three people. This water sports activity is quite user-friendly being a relaxed ride and considered ideal for families and solo riders.

The best locations to explore in Goa: Candolim, Colva, Rajbaga, Calangute, Miramar and Mobor.

Perfect season: October to March

Approximate Cost: Rs 600 – 1,200

3. Parasailing


Parasailing is also known as Parakiting or Parascending. This activity involves towing the parasailer behind the boat which is attached to a parachute-like canopy. The parasailer is then lifted into the air and glides through the sky as the parasail is hung from the harness that is fastened to the boat. During this activity, the parasailer would feel weightless and have the freedom to enjoy the beautiful view. The types of parasailing that we provide are solo parasailing where a single person can experience this, and Tandem parasailing where two people are attached to the same parasail which is ideal for couples, and families. There’s no special skill set required for this activity, just relax and enjoy the mesmerizing view from the top.

The best locations to explore in Goa: Anjuna Beach, Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Majorda Beach, Dona Paula Beach and Candolim Beach.

Perfect season: October to March

Approximate Cost: Rs 1,500 – 3,000

4. Flyboarding


Flyboarding is an exciting water sports activity in which a long hose connects a jet ski to the board the participant stands on. The jet ski supplies the momentum, impelling the rider into the air and allowing them to perform a range of ariel stunts. To be able to successfully enjoy flyboarding the essential requirements involve a jet ski, a flyboard, and a trained operator. This activity also requires participants to have a good level of fitness and coordination as it could physically demanding. It is crucial to obey all the safety requirements and instructions given by the operator to indulge into an enjoyable experience.

The best locations to explore in Goa:Chapora and Calangute Beach.

Perfect season: Early October to Early May.

Approximate Cost: Rs 2,500 – 4,000

5. Kayaking


Kayaking is a fun activity that involves using a double-bladed paddle in a small vessel which allows navigating through water. This activity is considered a low-impact activity that enhances aerobic fitness, flexibility, and strength. You can paddle on lakes, rivers, or even the sea. In addition, this activity can be experienced as a fun vacation, fun activity, or a hobby.

The best locations to explore in Goa: Baga Beach

Perfect season: October to March

Approximate Cost: Rs 1,000 – 2,000
