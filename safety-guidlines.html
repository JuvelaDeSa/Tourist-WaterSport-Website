Safety Guidlines

Guidlines for Experiencing Water Sports in Goa

1. Children above 12 and adults are allowed to participate in these water sports activities.
2. Planning the activities well in advance depending upon the suitable weather forecast can be beneficial.
3. Make sure to wear the right clothing during the activities.
4. Pick the right life jacket size that fits you well, as it is a crucial safety gear.
5. Always cooperate with the operator and follow their instructions.
6. Inform the instructor well in advance of any health issues involved, while booking the activities.
7. Make sure to prepare yourself physically and mentally to some extent.
